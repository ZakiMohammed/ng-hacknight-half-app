General Information

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where programmers, designers, and other tech enthusiasts come together to collaborate on software projects, typically within a short time frame, such as 24-48 hours.

Who can participate in the hackathon?

The hackathon is open to anyone with an interest in technology, including students, professionals, and hobbyists. No prior experience is necessary.

How do I register for the hackathon?

You can register for the hackathon by visiting our registration page and filling out the required information. Registration is free!


Event Details

When and where is the hackathon taking place?

The hackathon will be virtual.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Participants should bring their laptops, chargers, and any other necessary equipment. We will provide food, drinks, and a comfortable workspace.

Will there be prizes?

Yes, there will be prizes for the top teams. Details about the prizes will be announced closer to the event.


Support

How can I get help during the hackathon?

Mentors and organizers will be available throughout the event to provide support and answer any questions you may have.

What if I don't have a team?

If you don't have a team, don't worry! We will have a team formation session at the beginning of the hackathon to help you find teammates.
